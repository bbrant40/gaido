Welcome to Gaido

What is Gaido?

Gaido is like having a local show you around their city! A new app for travellers who want to become fully immersed in the cities they visit and learn more about each destination than what you'll find on GoogleMaps.

We offer self-guided tours, themed around art, food, culture and more. Explore completely at your own pace, skipping what doesn't interest you and finding out more about what does.

Gaido currently offers tours in London, Paris, Tokyo and over 25 other cities, with more being added every week.
